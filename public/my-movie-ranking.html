The Best Movies According to Alain

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Fargo

The Coen Bros are the best. Perfect film. Great cold snowy atmosphere.

The Truman Show

Great concept and executions. Very forward thinking in its ideas and great direction by Peter Weir. More relevant all the time.

Jurassic Park

The ultimate crowd pleasing summer blockbuster film. You cant make a dinosaur movie without the franchise. A great director teamed up with the most talented in visual effects artists and animatronics.
